\noindent
To the Editor(s),

\vskip 1em

We are pleased to submit a revised version of our manuscript,
``Parameterizing the genetic architecture under stabilizing selection.''

We are grateful for the time and care taken by the reviewers and editor.
Their comments helped us improve the clarity of both the theoretical development
and the empirical presentation.
In revising the manuscript, we focused on sharpening the exposition,
making the notation more consistent throughout,
and clarifying the interpretation of the simulation results.

In particular, the revision strengthens the presentation of the Results and Discussion,
improves the figure captions and appendix cross-references,
and makes the connection between the text and the estimation procedures used in the code more explicit.
We also made a number of targeted language and notation corrections throughout the manuscript
to improve readability without changing the substance of the work.

We believe that the revised manuscript is clearer, more self-contained,
and better positioned for readers interested in the relationship between
stabilizing selection, frequency-dependent genetic architectures,
and mixed-model inference.

We have included a point-by-point response to the reviewers
describing the revisions in detail.

\vskip 2em

\noindent
Sincerely,

\vskip 2em

Hanbin Lee, on behalf of the authors
